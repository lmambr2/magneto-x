# [¶](#magneto-x-cadstep-resource-center) Magneto X CAD/STEP Resource Center

## [¶](#introduction) Introduction

 Welcome to the Magneto X CAD/STEP Resource Center! This page is dedicated to providing the Magneto X community with comprehensive access to all the CAD and STEP files related to the physical structure of the Magneto X 3D printer. Whether you are looking to customize your printer, create new add-ons, or simply gain a deeper understanding of its intricate design, these files will serve as invaluable tools for all enthusiasts and developers.

## [¶](#accessing-the-files) Accessing the Files

 Below you will find links to download the CAD/STEP files for various components of the Magneto X. These files are detailed representations of the physical aspects of the printer, allowing for precise modifications and enhancements.

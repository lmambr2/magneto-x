How to replace Y axis | Peopoly Wiki - - - - - - - -

 This document mainly describes how to replace the Y-axis. Replacing the Y-axis involves two main steps:

- Remove the X-axis connectors

- Remove the Y-axis

Please note that all operations in this document should be performed with the printer's power disconnected.

 Strictly follow the steps in this document. Additionally, you can watch this

 [¶](#disassembly-video-for-more-details-httpswwwyoutubecomwatchv8nj7fy0eqvo) disassembly video for more details : [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8nJ7fy0Eqvo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8nJ7fy0Eqvo)

# [¶](#h-1-remove-the-x-axis) 1. Remove the X-axis

## [¶](#h-11-remove-the-y-axis-motor-cover-plate) 1.1 Remove the Y-axis motor cover plate

 Remove all screws from the Y-axis motor cover plate.

## [¶](#h-12-remove-the-eight-screws-near-the-screen-on-the-x-axis) 1.2 Remove the eight screws near the screen on the X-axis

 Remove all the screws highlighted in the figure below.

## [¶](#h-13-remove-the-x-axis-wiring-harness) 1.3 Remove the X-axis wiring harness

 First, remove the zip tie from the X-axis wiring harness:

 Then, loosen the three screws holding the wiring harness.

## [¶](#h-14-remove-the-four-screws-holding-the-x-axis-away-from-the-screen) 1.4 Remove the four screws holding the X-axis away from the screen

 After removing the wiring harness, gently move the harness to the left to reveal two screws holding the X-axis, as shown below. Loosen them.

 Go around to the front of the machine to see another two screws holding the X-axis, as shown in the image below.

## [¶](#h-15-disconnect-the-x-and-y-limit-switch-connectors) 1.5 Disconnect the X and Y limit switch connectors

 Note: When unplugging, pinch the plastic connector with both hands to pull it out. Do not pull directly on the wire as it may break.

## [¶](#h-16-remove-the-x-axis) 1.6 Remove the X-axis

 After removing the screws on both sides of the X-axis, use both hands to lift off the X-axis.

 Note, after removing the X-axis, there are two dowel pins on the side away from the screen that need to be reinserted during the next installation.

## [¶](#h-17-remove-the-x-axis-drag-chain-sheet-metal-screws) 1.7 Remove the X-axis drag chain sheet metal screws

After removing the sheet metal, lift the X-axis and the sheet metal from the Y-axis moving subassembly housing and place it aside.

## [¶](#h-25-start-removing-the-final-fixing-screws-on-both-sides-of-the-y-axis) 2.5 Start removing the final fixing screws on both sides of the Y-axis

 Remove the fixing screws of the Y-axis. Note, on each side, remove only three screws first and keep one screw to be removed last.

## [¶](#h-26-place-the-x-axis-and-sheet-metal-on-the-heated-bed) 2.6 Place the X-axis and sheet metal on the heated bed

 Refer to the details in the video: [https://youtu.be/8nJ7fy0Eqvo?t=136](https://youtu.be/8nJ7fy0Eqvo?t=136)

Place the entire X-axis on the heated bed.

## [¶](#h-27-remove-the-y-axis) 2.7 Remove the Y-axis

 For the final step, refer to the details in this video: [https://youtu.be/8nJ7fy0Eqvo?t=150](https://youtu.be/8nJ7fy0Eqvo?t=150)

 Hold the Y-axis with one hand and remove the last screw with the other hand.

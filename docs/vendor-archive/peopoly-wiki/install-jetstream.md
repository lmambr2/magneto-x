# [¶](#jetstream-installation-guide) Jetstream Installation Guide

## [¶](#h-1preparation) 1.Preparation

 Before starting the Jetstream installation, please ensure the printer is powered off.

## [¶](#h-2replacing-the-left-acrylic-panels) 2.Replacing the Left Acrylic Panels

 Remove the acrylic panels B1 and B2 on the left side by unscrewing the 3X6 flat round head screws using a pink wrench. Replace them with acrylic panels B3 and B4.

 Included in the enclosure kit, we provide two additional perforated acrylic panels (B3, B4), which are specifically designed for use with the Jetstream. For reference, see the enclosure setup guide Part 3.3:

[https://wiki.peopoly.net/en/magneto/magneto-x/enclosure-setup-guide](/en/magneto/magneto-x/enclosure-setup-guide)

Upon completion, the setup should look as shown in the diagram.

## [¶](#h-3securing-jetstream-to-the-left-side-of-the-printer) 3.Securing Jetstream to the Left Side of the Printer

 Use the cup head 3X6 screws provided and a green wrench to mount the side fan on the acrylic panel as illustrated.

## [¶](#h-4fan-wiring-installation) 4.Fan Wiring Installation

 Use an Allen wrench to slightly open the green insulating plate at the bottom, then insert the fan connector into the bottom of the printer.

 Remove the sheet metal cover on the bottom left side of the printer:

 Inside the casing, there are two connectors, one labeled LED and the other labeled FAN:

 Arrange the wires as shown in the diagram:

 Reattach the cover plate screws.

## [¶](#h-5testing) 5.Testing

 Power on the printer, navigate to Action → Fan, turn on the Jetstream, and test if the fan is spinning.

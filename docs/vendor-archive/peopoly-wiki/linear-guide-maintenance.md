Linear Guide Maintenance | Peopoly Wiki - - - - - - - -

 This document provides guidance on how to address issues such as jerky movement or rust on linear motor rails.

 We also recommend regular lubrication of the linear rails (once every 1.5 months).

## [¶](#h-1-items-to-prepare) 1. Items to Prepare

- WD-40 Rust Remover

- High-pressure grease

- Lint-free cloth

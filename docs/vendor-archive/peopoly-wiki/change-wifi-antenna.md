Guide to Replacing the WiFi Antenna on Magneto X for Enhanced Signal Quality | Peopoly Wiki - - - - - - - -

 This document explains how to replace the WiFi antenna on the Magneto X to improve WiFi signal quality.

## [¶](#h-1-preparation) 1. Preparation

 First, it is recommended to purchase the following types of antennas:

- Antenna extension head

- SMA connector

 Please choose this antenna:

[Antenna link on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/2-4ghz-Antenna-Connector-Female-Pigtail/dp/B07D1Z3W7T/ref=sr_1_12?crid=1L9MF8S9ZW2XM)

 Please find the Antenna-Mount on the list below:

[Peopoly Wiki - Magneto X Design Files](/en/magneto/magneto-x/magneto-x-design-files)

Download and print it out.

## [¶](#h-2-antenna-installation-and-fixation) 2. Antenna Installation and Fixation

 After printing the model, insert the antenna into the printed fixture.

Open the printer's control box cover:

Temporarily disconnect the HDMI and USB connected to the Linux board.

 Slightly lift the side of the board that connects to the antenna:

 Remove the antenna and then place it properly.

 Fix the removed antenna on the plastic clip:

 Arrange and secure the prepared antenna and its printed parts as shown in the diagram below:

 After fixing the antenna, reinsert the SMA port back onto the Linux board.

 After installation, remember to reconnect the previously removed HDMI and USB cables to their respective ports.

# [¶](#orcaslicer) OrcaSlicer

 The device parameters and material parameters of Magneto X can be found directly in Orca Slicer v2.1. Please download the latest Orca Slicer v2.1 directly:

[https://github.com/SoftFever/OrcaSlicer/releases/tag/v2.1.0](https://github.com/SoftFever/OrcaSlicer/releases/tag/v2.1.0)

 OrcaSlicer is a versatile open-source 3D slicing software, initially branched from Bambu Studio and ultimately tracing its origins to renowned slicers like Prusa Slicer, Super Slicer, and Slic3r. It stands out for offering an extensive range of printer presets, comprehensive print settings, and sophisticated tools, all aimed at refining the 3D printing process. Custom printer profiles, auto-calibrations, precise wall adjustments, and multi-plate options are among the key features, with a particular emphasis on compatibility with Klipper-based 3D printers. The software's design focuses on delivering a streamlined and efficient slicing experience, making it an excellent option for users who demand meticulous control over their 3D printing activities.

 Peopoly endorses OrcaSlicer as the recommended slicing software for the Magneto X, given its compatibility with the Klipper Firmware that the Magneto X utilizes. The software has been rigorously tested with the Magneto X, ensuring that it offers the most reliable printer and material profiles. Additionally, features like wireless printing and monitoring have been thoroughly tested. Peopoly is actively working to integrate the Magneto X into the official OrcaSlicer v2.0 release. Peopoly is also a proud sponsor for OrcaSlicer project.

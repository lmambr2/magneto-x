Reolace Y Axis Mover | Peopoly Wiki - - - - - - - -

 This document explains how to replace the Y-axis rotor.

 Before starting the replacement, please have your phone ready. When removing wires or arranging components, take a photo as a reference to avoid incorrect assembly during reinstallation.

## [¶](#h-1-removing-the-x-axis) 1. Removing the X-axis

 First, the X-axis needs to be removed.

 Remove the screws securing the Y-axis rotor housing.

 Remove the X-axis endstop.

 Detach the Y-axis mounting studs.

 Remove the X-axis securing screws and the Y-axis endstop.

 Remove the front securing screws of the X-axis.

 Remove the rear securing screws of the X-axis.

 Loosen the zip ties on the drag chain wires.

 Detach the drag chain wires from the toolhead.

 Remove the drag chain metal bracket.

 Disconnect all wiring from the Y-axis rotor.

 Take the X-axis drag chain wires and set them aside.

 Lift both sides of the X-axis gently.

## [¶](#h-2-removing-the-y-axis) 2. Removing the Y-axis

 Disconnect the Y-axis encoder terminal.

 Use cutting pliers to remove the securing pins on the X-axis gantry.

 Loosen the cable clamps at the base of the Y-axis rotor.

 From the base of the Y-axis rotor, unscrew the four securing screws.

 Once the four screws are loosened, remove the Y-axis rotor.

## [¶](#h-3-installation) 3. Installation

 Install the new Y-axis rotor using the method described in the previous step.

 For other components, follow the disassembly steps in reverse order to reassemble everything.
